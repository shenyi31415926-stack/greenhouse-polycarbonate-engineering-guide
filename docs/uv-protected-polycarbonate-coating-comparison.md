# UV-Protected Polycarbonate — Coating Technology Comparison

| Method | Lifespan | Manufacturers |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| Two-sided co-extrusion | 15-20yr | Covestro, Bakway |
| Single-sided co-extrusion | 10-15yr | Palram, Brett Martin |
| Surface coating | 7-10yr | Mid-tier |
| Bulk stabilizer | 3-7yr | Budget |

Co-extrusion bonds UV protection molecularly — cannot peel. Surface coating sits on top and degrades from abrasion and thermal cycling.

### Q: How long before yellowing?

Co-extruded: 10-15 years. No UV protection: 2-4 years.
