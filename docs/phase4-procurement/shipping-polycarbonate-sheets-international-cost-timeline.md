# Shipping Polycarbonate Sheets Internationally — Cost and Timeline

PC sheet shipping is volume-driven, not weight-driven — the panels take up container space but are relatively light. Understanding container optimization saves 20-30% on freight.

## Container Capacity by Panel Type

| Container | 10mm Multiwall | 16mm Multiwall | 6mm Solid |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|-----------|
| 20ft (~33 CBM) | 800-1,000 sqm | 500-650 sqm | 1,200-1,500 sqm |
| 40ft (~67 CBM) | 1,800-2,200 sqm | 1,100-1,400 sqm | 2,600-3,200 sqm |
| 40ft High Cube (~76 CBM) | 2,000-2,500 sqm | 1,300-1,600 sqm | 3,000-3,600 sqm |

## Packaging Requirements

- Panels must be shipped flat (never on edge — warps panels)
- Moisture barrier packaging (PC absorbs moisture in transit, causing extrusion bubbles during thermoforming)
- Edge protection (corner guards + foam between panels)
- Palletized with strapping (not loose-loaded)
- "This Side Up" and "Do Not Stack" markings

## Incoterms Explained

| Incoterm | Supplier Pays | Buyer Pays | Best For |
|----------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| EXW (Ex Works) | Nothing | Everything from factory gate | Experienced importers |
| FOB (Free On Board) | To vessel at origin port | Ocean freight + destination | Standard choice |
| CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight) | To destination port | Import duties + inland | First-time importers |
| DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) | Everything to your door | Nothing | Turnkey, highest cost |

## Q: Do PC sheets need special customs documentation?

Yes. HS Code 3920.61 (polycarbonate sheets, non-cellular) or 3920.69 (other). Certificate of Origin (Form A for GSP countries, Form F for China-ASEAN FTA). Manufacturer's declaration for anti-dumping compliance (some countries have AD duties on Chinese PC sheets — verify before ordering).
