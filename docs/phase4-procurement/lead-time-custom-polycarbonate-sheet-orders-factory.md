# Lead Time for Custom Polycarbonate Sheet Orders from Factory

Custom PC sheet orders follow a predictable timeline. Understanding the critical path prevents project delays.

## Lead Time Breakdown

| Phase | Standard Order | Custom Order | What Happens |
|-------|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| Order confirmation | 1-2 days | 2-3 days | PO review, technical spec confirmation |
| Raw material procurement | 3-7 days | 5-14 days | Resin ordering (Makrolon/Lexan 5-10 day lead from Covestro/SABIC) |
| Extrusion scheduling | 3-5 days | 7-14 days | Slot in production schedule |
| Extrusion run | 1-3 days | 2-5 days | Actual production (custom = slower, more QC) |
| QC and testing | 1-2 days | 2-3 days | Batch testing, optical/mechanical verification |
| Cutting/fabrication | 2-5 days | 3-7 days | CNC routing, edge finishing |
| Packaging | 1-2 days | 1-2 days | Moisture barrier, palletizing |
| **Factory total** | **12-26 days** | **22-48 days** | From order to ready-to-ship |
| Ocean freight | 15-35 days | 15-35 days | Depends on destination |
| Customs clearance | 2-5 days | 2-5 days | Import processing |
| Inland transport | 1-3 days | 1-3 days | Port to warehouse |
| **Total door-to-door** | **30-69 days** | **40-91 days** | From order to receipt |

## Factors That Extend Lead Time

| Factor | Additional Time | Why |
|--------|----------------|-----|
| Custom thickness | +7-14 days | Extrusion line changeover |
| Custom color/tint | +10-20 days | Color masterbatch ordering |
| Light-conversion additive | +5-10 days | Specialty compounding |
| Hard coat (both sides) | +3-5 days | Two coating passes |
| CNC fabrication | +3-7 days | Programming + cutting |
| Holiday periods | +14-21 days | Chinese New Year (Jan/Feb), Golden Week (Oct) |
| New mold/tooling | +21-35 days | Die design and fabrication |

## Rush Order Strategy

- **Pay the changeover fee:** $500-2,000 to bump your order ahead in the extrusion schedule
- **Accept standard resin:** Switch from custom Makrolon grade to whatever the factory has in stock (saves 5-10 days resin procurement)
- **Split the order:** Rush 30% by air freight (3-5 days, but 5-8x cost) to start your project; balance by sea
- **Pre-book slot:** For recurring orders, reserve production capacity 8-12 weeks ahead

## Air Freight: When It Makes Sense

Air freight for PC sheets costs $3-5/kg vs $0.15-0.30/kg for sea freight. Only use for: urgent replacement panels when downtime costs exceed freight premium, prototype/test panels for new projects, and small sample orders (< 50 sqm).
