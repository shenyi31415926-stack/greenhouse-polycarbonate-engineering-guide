# Minimum Order Quantity for Polycarbonate Sheets from Factory

MOQ is the first question every procurement manager asks — and the answer determines whether factory-direct sourcing is viable for your project.

## MOQ by Product Type

| Product | Standard MOQ | Custom MOQ | Notes |
|---------|-------------|------------|-------|
| Standard multiwall (clear, 6-16mm) | 50-100 sqm | — | Off-the-shelf |
| Standard solid (clear, 2-12mm) | 50-100 sqm | — | Off-the-shelf |
| Custom thickness multiwall | — | 200-500 sqm | Setup cost drives MOQ |
| Custom tint/color | — | 300-500 sqm | Color masterbatch minimum |
| Light-conversion PC | 100-200 sqm | 300+ sqm | Specialty additive cost |
| Anti-fog coated | 100-200 sqm | 300+ sqm | Coating line setup |
| Hard-coated sheet | 50-100 sqm | — | Standard product |
| Optical-grade sheet | 50-100 sqm | — | Premium but standard |
| Corrugated PC | 100-200 sqm | 500+ sqm | Die/mold cost |
| CNC-routed panels | 20-50 sqm | 50+ sqm | Labor-driven, not material |

## Why MOQs Exist

1. **Extrusion setup:** Changing thickness requires stopping the line, changing calibration tools — 2-4 hours of lost production
2. **Resin minimums:** Premium resins (Makrolon, Lexan) have supplier minimums per grade
3. **Color/tint:** Color masterbatch minimum purchase is typically 25kg — enough for 500-800 sqm
4. **Coating line:** Anti-fog or special coating requires dedicated coating line setup

## How to Negotiate Lower MOQ

- **Combine with stock orders:** Ask if your custom run can be combined with a standard production batch
- **Accept overrun:** Agree to purchase the full minimum resin batch (you get extra inventory at cost)
- **Pay the setup fee:** Offer to pay the extrusion line changeover cost ($500-2,000) in exchange for lower MOQ
- **Samples first:** Order 5-10 sqm as "samples" for testing before committing to MOQ

## Q: Can I split MOQ across multiple products?

Sometimes. If the products share the same base resin and thickness (different widths or cut sizes), they can be produced in the same extrusion run. Ask: "Can I combine MOQ across SKUs that share the same base material?"
