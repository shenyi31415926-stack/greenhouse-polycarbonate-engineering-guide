# How to Source Polycarbonate from China — Step by Step

1. **Verify certification** — Check IATF/ISO certificate numbers
2. **Request samples** — Test thickness, UV, light transmission
3. **Factory audit** — In-person or SGS/TÜV third-party ($1,500-3,000)
4. **Negotiate terms** — 30% deposit + 70% pre-shipment; FOB Shanghai standard
5. **Pre-shipment QC** — Random 5-10% panel inspection
6. **Shipping** — 20ft container ≈ 1,000 sqm; 25-35 days to Europe

## Red Flags
- Cannot provide verifiable certificate numbers
- Refuses to send samples
- Price > 30% below market
- No export references to your region
