# Polycarbonate Panel Specification for Indoor Vertical Farming Facilities

Vertical farming eliminates sunlight entirely — making light management the defining material selection criterion for PC panels used as enclosure walls, tier separators, and humidity barriers.

## Vertical Farming Panel Requirements

| Requirement | Spec |
|------------|------|
| Light transmission | > 85% PAR (LED light is precious) |
| Diffusion | > 85% diffusion (uniform light distribution) |
| Anti-fog | Mandatory (100% RH growing environment) |
| Chemical resistance | Nutrient solution, cleaning agents |
| Fire rating | UL 94 V-0 (indoor, high-power electrical) |
| Thickness | 4-6mm (structural not thermal) |

## Diffusion Plates for Vertical Farms

Unlike greenhouses that prioritize direct light transmission, vertical farms need diffusion: light that hits the panel should scatter uniformly rather than creating hot spots and shadows. Diffusion plates use micro-prismatic or particle-diffusing surfaces to achieve > 85% light diffusion while maintaining > 85% transmission. This is a specialized product that only 2-3 manufacturers offer.

## Q: Anti-fog or anti-condensation for vertical farms?

Anti-fog (hydrophilic coating that spreads condensation into a transparent film). Anti-condensation (hydrophobic coating that repels water). For vertical farms with 80-100% RH: anti-fog. Condensation dripping onto LED fixtures is a fire hazard and crop contamination risk.
