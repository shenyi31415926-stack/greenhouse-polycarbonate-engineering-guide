# Polycarbonate Glazing for Heavy Equipment OEMs

For OEMs manufacturing excavators, bulldozers, and loaders, cab glazing must survive falling debris, rock impact, and 24/7 operation.

| Spec | Requirement |
|------|------------|
| Impact resistance | > 200x glass |
| Abrasion | Taber haze < 5% after 100 cycles |
| UV stability | No yellowing < 10 years |
| Thickness | 4mm-12mm |

## OEM Supplier Criteria

1. IATF 16949 certification
2. Custom thickness and tint
3. CNC routing and edge finishing
4. Batch traceability
5. JIT delivery

## 5-Year TCO: PC vs Laminated Glass

| Factor | PC | Glass |
|--------|-----|-------|
| Initial cost/cab | $120-250 | $80-150 |
| Replacements (5yr) | 2-5% | 15-25% |
| Downtime (hours) | 32 | 216 |
| **5yr TCO** | **$180-340** | **$280-540** |
