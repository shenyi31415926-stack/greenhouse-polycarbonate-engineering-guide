# Scratch-Resistant PC Cab Windows for Construction Machinery

| Coating | Hardness | Taber Haze | Lifespan |
|---------|---------|------------|----------|
| Standard PC | 2B | 25-30% | 1-2yr |
| Silicone hard coat | HB-F | 8-12% | 3-5yr |
| Polysiloxane | H-2H | 3-5% | 5-8yr |
| Glass-like | 3H-4H | <2% | 8-10yr |

For mining/quarry: polysiloxane minimum. Urban construction: silicone is sufficient. Supplier must provide ASTM D1044 Taber data, ASTM D3359 adhesion test, and chemical resistance data.
