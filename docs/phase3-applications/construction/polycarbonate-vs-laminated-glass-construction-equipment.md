# PC vs Laminated Glass for Construction Equipment — TCO

20-unit excavator fleet, 5 years:

| Category | PC | Glass |
|----------|-----|-------|
| Initial | $4,800 | $3,200 |
| Replacements | $960 | $7,200 |
| Downtime cost | $4,800 | $32,400 |
| **5yr TCO** | **$10,960** | **$45,500** |

PC saves 76% over 5 years. Key factor: PC takes impact and remains functional; glass replacement means downtime.
