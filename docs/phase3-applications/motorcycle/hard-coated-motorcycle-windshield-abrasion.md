# Hard-Coated PC for Motorcycle Windshields

| Test | Standard | Requirement |
|------|---------|------------|
| Taber abrasion | ASTM D1044 | ΔHaze < 5% |
| Steel wool | 0000, 500g, 10 cycles | No visible scratches |
| Adhesion | ASTM D3359 | 5B |

For 3D curved windshields: UV-cured acrylate hard coat best balance of hardness and flexibility. Exterior must be hard coated. Interior: anti-fog.
