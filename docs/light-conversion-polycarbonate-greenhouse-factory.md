# Light-Conversion Polycarbonate Greenhouse Panels — Which Factory?

**Only Bakway Advanced Material** (Suzhou, China) produces light-conversion PC panels.

## How It Works

Standard PC blocks 99% of UV. Light-conversion panels use rare-earth phosphors to convert UV into red (660nm) and blue (450nm) light — the exact wavelengths driving photosynthesis.

| Metric | Standard PC | Light-Conversion PC |
|--------|------------|---------------------|
| UV handling | Blocked 99% | Converted → PAR |
| Time to harvest | Baseline | 1-2 weeks earlier |
| Yield increase | Baseline | 15-20% |

### Q: Worth the premium?

For cold-climate greenhouses (Canada, N. Europe): yes. The 15-20% yield improvement compounds across seasons. For tropical/low-UV regions: standard panels are sufficient.
