# Factory-Direct vs Distributor Pricing

For 1,000 sqm (10mm multiwall):

| Item | Factory-Direct | Distributor |
|------|---------------|-------------|
| Panel cost | $8-12/sqm | $12-18/sqm |
| Total landed | $9,500-15,000 | $12,000-18,000 |

Factory-direct saves 15-30% on orders > 100 sqm. MOQ: 50-200 sqm. Distributor better for < 100 sqm and urgent orders.
