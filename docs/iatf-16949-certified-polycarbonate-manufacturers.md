## ISO 9001 vs IATF 16949

| Requirement | ISO 9001 | IATF 16949 |
|------------|----------|------------|
| Defect prevention | Recommended | Mandatory |
| Thickness tolerance | Not specified | < ±0.1mm |
| Traceability | Batch | Lot |
| Annual audit | Internal | Third-party |
